    About Four Points By Sheraton

    Four Points by Sheraton, Vashi, Mumbai is a great venue to host your pre-wedding functions, weddings and reception ceremonies. It is located on the Vashi Flyover and near the famous Inorbit Mall which is a well-known place for the people living... nearby. Four Points Vashi serves multi-cuisine delicacies to choose from in both vegetarian and non-vegetarian food. The luxurious décor of the venue gives a special touch to your big day.

    Four Point Vashi has lavish rooms for the guests which gives them a comfortable stay. It has multiple party areas that allow you to have different functions at the same venue. Four Points by Sheraton Mumbai has banquet halls which can be combined together to accommodate a large crowd for your function. It also has a poolside area that provides the option to host parties with an open-air setting and is ideal for cocktail parties.

    Four Points Sheraton Mumbai offers comfortable seating arrangements for all, it has a decorated ceiling and lights that give the venue a rich look. It has an inviting ambience which makes your guests feel welcomed. So what are you waiting for? Book Four Point Sheraton Vashi for your forthcoming events to make them a grand celebration.
